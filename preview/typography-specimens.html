Four generations of the Mancuso family have poured their hearts into The Bootlegger Bistro. What began as a prohibition-era backroom on the outskirts of Las Vegas has grown into a South Strip landmark where locals and legends alike pull up a chair.

Frank Sinatra held court in the corner booth. Anthony Bourdain filmed here. The red sauce recipe hasn't changed since Mama Maria first wrote it down — and nobody’s asked her to.

Weight & Leading Contrast

This is DM Sans at weight 400 — slightly more presence for emphasized prose, subheads, and navigation labels. Notice how the line height opens the colour of the paragraph.

DM Sans 500 tightens up for button labels, table headers, and active states. Small caps are available via font-variant: small-caps for kickers and abbreviations.
